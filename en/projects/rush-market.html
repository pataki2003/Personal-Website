A Unity-based prototype built around business management and order handling. My main responsibility is developing connected gameplay systems and player interactions that work together as a coherent flow.

The project

Rush Market is a slower-paced business management game where the player fulfills orders, handles products, restocks shelves, prepares packages, and hands them over to couriers.

Technical thinking

One of the main challenges was making sure the gameplay systems do not behave like isolated features, but as parts of one connected game flow. Picking up products, inventory, orders, packing, and handoff all build on the same data models and states.
