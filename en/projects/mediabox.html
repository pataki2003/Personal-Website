Business Website

MEDIABOX

A modern, responsive website built for a nationwide pizza-box advertising service. The goal was to communicate the offer clearly to advertisers and pizzeria partners while supporting quote requests and contact paths.

Live project Business website Lead generation

Product preview

The problem

The service speaks to two different audiences. Advertisers are looking for advertising space, while pizzerias are looking for a partner opportunity. The website needed to make the offer quickly understandable for both groups.

The solution

The website presents the service through separate communication blocks, clear calls to action, and structured content. The focus is on quote requests, the partner program, and the business value of the service.

Key features

  • Separate content for advertisers and pizzerias
  • Quote request calls to action
  • Responsive layout
  • Service presentation sections
  • Partner program presentation
  • Contact options
  • SEO-based content structure

Outcome

The result is a visually consistent, easy-to-understand, business-focused website that supports both advertiser quote requests and pizzeria partner acquisition.
